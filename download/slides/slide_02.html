PART 2 · PRODUCT SOLUTION (1/2)

Paste a JD and a batch of resumes. Get a ranked, explained shortlist in under a minute.

The recruiter stays in control throughout — ScreenWise recommends with reasoning, never auto-rejects.

settings Product workflow
1
Paste JD + resumes
Recruiter pastes the job description and a batch of resumes (separated by --- ). No login, no setup.
2
AI extracts the JD brief
Must-haves, nice-to-haves, seniority signals, and domain context — parsed into a structured rubric.
3
Score on a 5-signal rubric
Each candidate gets a 0-100 fitment score with per-signal reasoning. Schema-constrained JSON output, not free text.
4
Ranked dashboard + outreach
Ranked cards with matched/missing skills, red flags, and a personalized outreach email per candidate — ready to copy.
compare_arrows User journey: before vs. after
BEFORE
Manual screening · ~5 hrs per role
  • Open 200-resume ATS folder, read each (~90s each)
  • Flag ~30 maybes based on gut feel
  • Sync with hiring manager to debate the shortlist
  • Manually draft outreach — generic templates
AFTER
ScreenWise-assisted · ~45 min per role
  • Paste JD + 200 resumes, click Run
  • 60 seconds later: ranked shortlist of top 15, each with written reasoning
  • Review red flags and strengths, adjust shortlist in one click
  • Copy personalized outreach drafts, send from own inbox
Explainable AI Every ranked candidate comes with matched skills, missing skills, key strengths, and red flags — written in plain English.
