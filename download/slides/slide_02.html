PART 2 · PRODUCT SOLUTION (1/2)

ScreenWise — paste a JD and a batch of resumes, get a ranked shortlist in under a minute.

No logins, no setup. The recruiter stays in control — ScreenWise recommends, never auto-rejects.

Product workflow

1
Paste JD + resumes
Recruiter pastes the job description and a batch of resumes (separated by --- on their own line). No file upload needed for the prototype.
2
AI parses the JD
Extracts must-haves, nice-to-haves, seniority signals, and domain context into a structured brief.
3
Score on a 5-signal rubric
Each candidate gets a 0–100 fitment score with per-signal reasoning (skills, experience, seniority, domain, trajectory).
4
Ranked dashboard + outreach
Recruiter sees a ranked list with red flags, skill gaps, and a personalized outreach email per candidate — ready to copy.

Key features

list_checks
Rubric
5 calibrated signals, weighted: Skills 40 · Experience 25 · Seniority 15 · Domain 10 · Trajectory 10.
eye
Explainable
Every score comes with matched skills, missing skills, strengths, and red flags. No black boxes.
flag
Red-flag surfacing
Job-hopping, scope mismatch, gaps — surfaced for the recruiter, never used to auto-reject.
mail
Personalized outreach
One-click email draft that references a SPECIFIC detail from that candidate's resume.
shield
Bias guardrails
Name-blind scoring option. Strong Fit (≥80) is intentionally rare — most candidates land in Possible or Pass.
