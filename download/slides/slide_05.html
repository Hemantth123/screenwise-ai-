What it does

  • Paste a JD + paste resumes (one per block, separated by ---)
  • Click "Run AI fitment analysis"
  • Get ranked cards: score, recommendation, matched/missing skills, key strengths, red flags, one-line summary, personalized outreach email
  • Copy outreach email in one click, mark Shortlist or Pass per candidate
layers

Stack

Next.js 16 TypeScript Tailwind shadcn/ui z-ai-web-dev-sdk (server-side) No database (stateless prototype)
