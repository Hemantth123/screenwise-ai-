PART 2 · PRODUCT SOLUTION (2/2)

A recruiter's Tuesday afternoon, before and after ScreenWise.

schedule

User journey: before vs. after

BEFORE
Manual screening, 5 hours per role
  • Open 200-resume ATS folder, read each (~90s each = ~5 hours)
  • Flag ~30 maybes based on gut feel
  • Sync with hiring manager to debate the shortlist
  • Manually draft outreach to 10 candidates — generic templates
AFTER
ScreenWise-assisted, 45 minutes per role
  • Paste JD + 200 resumes, click Run
  • 60 seconds later: ranked shortlist of top 15, with explanations
  • Review red flags and strengths, adjust shortlist in one click
  • Copy personalized outreach drafts, send from own inbox
help_outline

Assumptions

SCOPE OF V0.1
  • Resumes arrive as text (paste or ATS export). PDF parsing is a v1.1 problem.
  • One recruiter owns one role at a time. Multi-recruiter collaboration is post-MVP.
  • The JD has at least a paragraph of real content. Two lines won't help anyone.
  • Outreach goes through the recruiter's inbox — ScreenWise drafts, never sends.
